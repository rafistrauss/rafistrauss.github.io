One of my favorite projects while working at RubensteinTech was the website for Cravath's Bicentennial celebration. The unique designs created some very interesting challenges and requirements.
Chief amongst these may have been the "gifs" that are sprinkled throughout the site; judicious use of the native video element allowed for much smaller files for the same quality, providing a seamless experience.
